Do's

There are certain Dos & Don’ts to follow while purchasing, bursting and storing crackers. Thus, it is very important to follow the precautions while bursting crackers.

Display fireworks as per the warnings and instructions mentioned on the pack
Buy fireworks directly from manufacturer or from authorized dealer only
Use fireworks only in outdoor
Always follow the safety tips marked on the fireworks
Use an agarbatti to ingnite the fireworks
Store fireworks in a cool & dry place
Always have adult supervision
Always wear eye protection when lightening fireworks
Light one fireworks at a time
Keep a bucket of water or a garden hose handy in case of fire or other mishap

Don't Do's

A little negligence, ignorance and carelessness can cause a fatal injury. It is very important to follow the precautions while bursting crackers.

Never make your own fireworks
Never try to re-light or pick up fireworks that have not ignited fully
Never carry fireworks in your packets
Never shoot fireworks in metal or glass containers
Never point or throw fireworks at another person
Do not wear loose clothing while using fireworks
After fireworks display, never pick up fireworks that may be left over as they may still be active
